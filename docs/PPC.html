Children's Privacy Policy

UAB "Travel Union" (hereinafter - " TU " or " we ") is an electronic money institution, company registration number 305106882, which has an electronic money institution license of the Bank of Lithuania no. LB001966, whose registered office is Saulėtekio al. 17, Vilnius.

TU, our mobile application, provides a forum for all family members, including children between the ages of 7 and 18 (" Children " or " You ") to use TU Wallet and/or TU Card, pay for things and learn how to use money.

Your parent or legal guardian who looks after you (“ Parent ”) has opened a TU account and/or a TU card for you.

Parents can check how you're using your account, where and how much you're spending, and how much money you have in your account (so they know when to top it up). Using the TU mobile app will teach you financial responsibility, money management skills, not to mention that the TU Wallet and TU Card are much safer than using cash.

Before you start using TU Wallet and/or TU Card, we would like to explain how we collect and use your Personal Data.

Most importantly, your parents can see your personal data and are responsible for your account. TU may also see your personal data because we need to use it to manage your account.

If you have any questions about your Personal Data and how we look after it, please email us at dpo@mytu.co or consult your parents.

A. What personal data we collect about you

What do we mean by "personal data"? Personal data is information we hold about you. This includes things like your name, date of birth, mother's name, father's name and email address and phone number. These are just a few examples of what your data is. There is a lot of other data that we don't use.

You can sign up (with certain restrictions and parental permission) to explore the TU mobile app, use the TU Wallet and/or Card to pay for items, and learn financial literacy skills at the same time. But in order for you to do this, we need to collect certain information about you.

When your parents opened a TU Wallet and/or TU Card, they gave us your information. We need this information to set up your account and allow you to use the TU Wallet and/or TU Card. In addition, we are required by law to collect and store some of your data.

TU collects only limited information about you as follows:

  • Name surname;
  • Identity card or passport;
  • Birth certificate;
  • Date, month and year of birth;
  • Telephone number;
  • Email address;
  • Your parent's email address, phone number we use to request parental consent;
  • Custodian documents;
  • Content of correspondence;
  • Payment information such as date, amount, location, store where the payment was made;
  • Face image.

Use of anonymous information. If the information collected from you does not identify you, or does not allow us to contact you or your device, we may use and disclose it for any purpose to the extent permitted by applicable law.

No ads! We do not serve ads based on information collected from you.

No newsletters ! Our email newsletters are for adults only - they are not tailored or directed at you. You should not attempt to sign up for email newsletters.

Permanent identifiers. When you interact with the TU Mobile App, certain technical information may be collected automatically to make the TU Mobile App more interesting and useful, for tracking analytics, spam protection, and various internal purposes related to our business. However, we do not collect any of this information from you. Analytics and personalization cookies are also disabled.

What happens when you or your parents do not provide the necessary information about you or withdraw consent? When we need your information because we are required to do so by law or we have to enter into a contract with your parents, we may prevent you from using the TU mobile app. Similarly, if we need your parental consent to process your data and your parent withdraws consent, we may not be able to provide you with certain services or features.

B. Why We Collect Information About You

We only collect information about you when it is necessary. There are many reasons for using your personal data:

  • so that we can create an account for you and allow you to use the TU mobile app;
  • determine your age;
  • allow you to use TU Wallet and/or TU Card to pay for items you purchase;
  • to answer your questions or provide you with requested information;
  • verify that the account is being used by you;
  • send a welcome pack and information such as tips on how to use your account to save money;
  • to analyze and improve the services we offer;
  • handle any feedback or complaints;
  • ensure security in the TU mobile app;
  • where we are required or permitted by law.

Limitation of purpose. We will only use your data for the purposes stated, unless we reasonably believe that it needs to be used for another reason and that reason is compatible with the original purpose. If we need to use your data for an unrelated purpose, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you.

We only collect your information if your parents give consent, your parents have signed a contract with us, we are required to do so by law, or if we have a legitimate interest in improving our services.

C. How and why is information about you shared?

Sometimes we need to disclose your information to third parties if we have your parent's permission or if we are required to do so by any applicable law or as part of the performance of a contract.

Here are some examples of who we share your information with:

  • other companies that help us operate or manage the TU Mobile App;
  • other people or companies who help us to ensure that we comply with the law and act fairly (such as lawyers and accountants);
  • other companies if they buy our company;
  • official people who monitor our activities to make sure we are looking after you properly;
  • our other companies.

D. How can you use our mobile app?

Your parents must already be TU customers in order to have a TU Wallet and/or TU Card. You can download the TU mobile app only with parental consent and personal information.

To set up your account on the TU mobile app:

  • Your parents must register you through their account on the TU mobile app; read and sign the contract on your behalf; submit your Personal data, guardianship documents and sign an electronic consent form for the processing of your Personal data. Only then can you download the TU mobile app, fill out the registration forms and complete the registration process.

In all cases, in order to download and access the TU mobile app, you must obtain parental consent to process your Personal Data.

In the case of children's accounts, if all the forms and confirmations mentioned above are submitted/confirmed by one of the parents, the contract must be valid and legally binding.

If your parents give us consent at this stage, TU will carry out the activity for which consent was required. If not, we will not provide any services.

E. How can your parents access, change or delete information about you?

Deleting your information and account.

If your parents:

  • want us to stop collecting information from or about you;
  • believe that you are participating in activities on the TU mobile app that use your personal information without their consent;
  • want us to no longer use and/or delete personal information we have collected from you online;
  • no longer wish you to have a TU Account and/or a TU Wallet and/or Card,

TU will delete your profile and any personal information that can be deleted upon request.

Your parents can exercise these rights through our Data Protection Officer by emailing dpo@mytu.co.

Accessing or changing your account and any personal information we collect.

Your parents can at any time:

  • access or modify your TU Account or TU Card;
  • change the personal information we have collected about you;

by clicking on the "My Account" link or by contacting us at support@mytu.co or by writing to us at the address below.

If a parent has any questions or concerns about their child's use of the TU Mobile App, we encourage parents to contact us at dpo@mytu.co.

F. What are your rights?

Information. You have the right to be informed about how we use your Personal Data.

Access. You have the right to request to see any personal data we hold about you.

Correction. You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete Personal Data we hold about you.

Deletion. You have the right to request that we delete all of your Personal Data.

Objection. You have the right to object to any of the reasons for which we collect your Personal Data.

Limitation. You have the right to request that we stop processing your Personal Data in certain situations (for example, to find out if your information is correct).

Portability. You have the right to request that we provide your Personal Data to you or another company.

Withdraw consent. You and your parents have the right to withdraw your consent for any processing based on it as a legal basis.

How to exercise these rights? These rights can be exercised using our TU mobile app. There is no charge for this. We may need to ask you for more information related to your inquiry to help us confirm that it is you. TU will review your request and provide a response within one month of receiving your request. In some cases, we may need more time to respond to your request.

The right to complain. We would like to be able to resolve any questions, requests or complaints we receive from you or your parents. However, if you or your parents believe that we could not help you, your parents can contact their local data protection supervisory authority https://vdai.lrv.lt/lt/veiklos-sritys-1/skundu-nagrinejimas.

G. International Transfers

Our headquarters is in the Republic of Lithuania. The information we collect from and about you will be stored and processed in the European Union and may not be stored or processed in other countries. However, it is our policy to ensure that appropriate contractual or other safeguards are in place for Personal Data transferred outside the European Union where required by European data protection legislation. If you have any questions about the safeguards applied to Children's personal data, you can contact us at dpo@mytu.co.

H. Personal Data from Third Party Sources

We do not collect any information about you from third parties or publicly available sources other than you and your parents (where they choose to provide it to us).

I. How long can we store your personal data?

We must store your personal data for the following periods:

  • as long as your parental consent is valid;
  • we will keep your data for up to ten years if you close the TU Wallet.

We may need to keep your Personal Data for longer if the law requires us to, or if we need to use it in a court case because someone or a company claims we have broken the law but we believe we have not. So even if you ask us to destroy your Personal Data, we may not always be able to do so immediately.

J. Get in touch

If you have any questions about your Personal Data, please email us at dpo@mytu.co or support@mytu.co Your parents can also contact us about your account.
